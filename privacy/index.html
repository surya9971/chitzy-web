Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Chitzy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Chitzy mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using Chitzy, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Phone number (used for OTP verification via Firebase)
  • Display name (chitzy_name), real name (optional)
  • Age, gender, state, city, work type, workplace (optional profile fields)

Usage Data

  • Messages sent and received (stored anonymously)
  • Streak activity and daily message counts
  • Profile visits (count only, not individual visitors)
  • In-app notification preferences

Device Information

  • Expo push notification token (to deliver notifications)
  • Device type (iOS / Android) for notification delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Chitzy account
  • To deliver anonymous messages between users
  • To send push notifications (new messages, reactions, streak reminders)
  • To maintain your streak and activity stats
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To process payments for Chitzy Pro (via Razorpay)

3. Anonymity

Chitzy is built around anonymous messaging. Sender identities are never revealed to recipients unless the recipient uses a hint (coins or ad). Even then, only a partial city-level hint is shown for Pro users. We never expose the sender's phone number or real name.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Phone number OTP authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Database and backend. Data stored on servers in the United States. Privacy Policy
  • Expo — Push notification delivery. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — In-app advertising (free users). May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation. Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay — Payment processing for Chitzy Pro. We do not store card or UPI details. Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your messages, profile data, and activity history are permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Chitzy is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security on our database, and authenticated API access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Update your profile information anytime in the app
  • Deletion — Request account deletion by emailing us
  • Opt-out — Disable notifications anytime in Profile → Settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of Chitzy after changes means you accept the updated policy.
